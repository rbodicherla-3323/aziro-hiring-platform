Follow these steps to use the Dashboard efficiently:

  1. Set timeframe: Use date pills (or custom date) to load metrics for the period you want.
  2. Choose section: Use the four detail buttons (Period Overview, Performance, Cost Savings, My Statistics).
  3. Open detail view: Click a button to load that section's full detailed panel below.
  4. Track trends: In Performance, review latest tests/completions and trend percentages.
  5. Check savings: In Cost Savings, monitor interview count, per-interview savings, and total savings.
  6. Review personal metrics: In My Statistics, use period filters and navigation arrows for your own results.
